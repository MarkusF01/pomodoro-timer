The Pomodoro Technique is a time-management method that uses short work intervals and breaks to enhance productivity, focus, and reduce stress.
Interval:
  25 minutes of work;
  5 minutes of rest;
  25 minutes of work;
  5 minutes of rest;
  25 minutes of work;
  5 minutes of rest;
  25 minutes of work;
  15 minutes of rest;
